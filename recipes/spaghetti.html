Yummy spaghetti!

Ingredients

Key Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion, garlic and spices

Steps

  1. Bring a pan of water to the boil and season it with salt.
  2. Add the spaghetti and stir it gently until all the strands sink beneath the water level.
  3. Cook following the pack instructions but taking 2 mins off the time suggested.
